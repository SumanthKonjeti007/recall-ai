About recall.ai

What is recall.ai?

recall.ai is an intelligent member lookup system that answers natural-language questions about your members. Simply ask what you want to know, and recall.ai will search through member conversations, preferences, and activities to find the answer.

How to Use recall.ai

Type your question in plain English using member names. For example:

"When is Layla traveling to London?"
"What are Vikram's favorite restaurants?"
"What has Sophia booked this month?"

What Data is Stored?

recall.ai searches through member messages and extracts:

  • Bookings: Restaurants, hotels, flights, events, experiences
  • Preferences: Seating choices, dietary needs, room types
  • Requests: Service inquiries, special arrangements
  • Timeline: When activities were booked or mentioned

Example Questions

"Where is Armand traveling next?"
"What are Fatima's dining preferences?"
"Which members visited Paris last month?"
"What events did Lorenzo book?"

Tips for Best Results

  • Use member names in your questions
  • Ask about specific activities, preferences, or bookings
  • Include time periods when searching for activities
  • Keep questions clear and conversational
