About Aurora

What is Aurora?

Aurora is your intelligent concierge assistant that helps you access and analyze client conversation data. It provides instant insights into client preferences, requests, and interactions.

What data does Aurora access?

  • Client conversations and messages
  • Preferences for experiences (museums, dining, travel)
  • Service requests and bookings
  • Temporal data (dates, time periods)
  • Client profiles and interaction history

Key Capabilities

  • Client Discovery: Find clients by their preferences or requests
  • Temporal Queries: Search by specific dates or time periods
  • Preference Analysis: Identify trends and patterns in client interests
  • Service Tracking: Monitor luxury service inquiries and bookings

Example Queries

"Which clients requested a private tour of the Louvre?"
"Show me clients interested in Michelin-starred restaurants"
"What requests did we receive in March 2025?"
"Find clients who prefer luxury vehicles"

Tips for Best Results

  • Be specific about what you're looking for
  • Include dates or time periods when relevant
  • Ask about preferences, requests, or specific services
  • Use natural language - Aurora understands context
